# PHASE 2 Strategic Design

## Cloud Architecture Definition
Cloud architecture refers to the components and services required to support the cloud computing model. 

### Key Components:
- **Front-end platform**: The interface for users to access the cloud services.
- **Back-end platform**: This includes the servers, storage, and databases that support the application.
- **Network**: The internet and internal network infrastructure that connects the front-end and back-end.
- **Cloud Services**: The various services offered to users, such as IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS.

### Benefits of Cloud Architecture:
- Scalability
- Flexibility
- Cost-Effectiveness

## AI Modernization PoV
AI Modernization involves leveraging AI technologies to enhance business processes and service delivery.

### Features:
- **Data Utilization**: Effectively harnessing data through AI for better decision-making.
- **Advanced Analytics**: Using machine learning algorithms to predict outcomes and trends.
- **Automated Processes**: Streamlining operations through intelligent automation.

### Challenges:
- Insufficient data

## Business Case TCO Modeling
Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) modeling provides a comprehensive estimate of the direct and indirect costs related to deploying a solution.

### Components of TCO:
- **Direct Costs**: Initial investment, operational expenses, maintenance, and support.
- **Indirect Costs**: Downtime, inefficiencies, and opportunity costs.

### Steps to Develop a TCO Model:
1. Identify all cost components
2. Gather data on costs
3. Analyze and forecast costs over a predetermined period
