# PHASE-0: Presales Foundation

## Overview
The Presales phase establishes the foundation for the Application Modernization Advisory engagement. This phase focuses on understanding customer needs, defining the scope of work, and preparing comprehensive proposals and statements of work for the modernization initiative.

## Objectives
- Conduct customer discovery and align on modernization drivers
- Develop detailed estimation and work breakdown structures
- Create executive presentations and customer-facing materials
- Establish project governance and team structure
- Define success criteria and service delivery approach

## Key Deliverables

### 1. **Sales & Engagement Materials**
- **Customer Deck** (EN/ES): Executive presentation for customer engagement
- **Success Cases**: Reference cases demonstrating previous modernization successes
- **Service Deck**: Overview of AI Modernization Advisory services
- **Journey Presentation**: Visual representation of the modernization journey

### 2. **Proposal & Contract Documents**
- **Statement of Work (SOW)** (EN/ES): Detailed scope, timeline, and deliverables
- **Proposal** (EN/ES): Comprehensive proposal for customer review and sign-off
- **Estimation Tool** (v1.2 & v2.0): Sizing and cost estimation calculators

### 3. **Planning & Organization**
- **Work Breakdown Structure (WBS)**: Detailed project scope decomposition
- **PoV WBS**: Proof of Concept work breakdown
- **RACI Matrix**: Roles and responsibilities definition
- **App Inventory**: Application assessment and categorization

## Key Outputs by Customer Interaction

### Initial Discovery Meeting
1. Confirm modernization drivers and business objectives
2. Review application inventory and current architecture
3. Align on scope, timeline, and investment approach

### Proposal Presentation
1. Present estimation results and cost implications
2. Discuss team composition and engagement model
3. Review SOW and delivery schedule

### Contract Execution
1. Execute SOW and secure stakeholder approval
2. Establish governance framework (RACI)
3. Kick-off date confirmation

## Process Flow

```
Customer Inquiry
    ↓
Initial Discovery (App Inventory, Drivers)
    ↓
Estimation & Analysis (Tools, Effort, Cost)
    ↓
Proposal Development (Scope, Timeline, Investment)
    ↓
Customer Presentation (Deck, Cases, Journey)
    ↓
SOW Finalization & Contract
    ↓
Kick-off → PHASE-1: Discovery & Assessment
```

## Success Criteria
- ✅ Clear definition of modernization scope and objectives
- ✅ Aligned cost and effort estimates
- ✅ Executive sponsor commitment
- ✅ Signed SOW and project governance
- ✅ Transition to Phase 1 with established project team

## Next Phase
After Presales completion, the engagement transitions to **PHASE-1: Discovery & Assessment**, where detailed technical and organizational discovery is conducted.
