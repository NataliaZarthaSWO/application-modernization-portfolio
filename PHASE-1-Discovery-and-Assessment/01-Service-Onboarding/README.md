# 01. Service Onboarding Module

## Overview
The Service Onboarding module establishes the foundation for the entire Application Modernization engagement. This module focuses on aligning stakeholders, defining project governance, and understanding the customer's modernization drivers and strategic objectives.

## Key Objectives
- Establish project governance and team structure
- Conduct strategic alignment workshops with leadership
- Document modernization drivers and business objectives
- Complete infrastructure baseline questionnaire
- Establish communication and decision-making framework
- Prepare for Phase 1 technical discovery activities

## Scope of Work

### 1. Kick-Off Meeting
- Executive sponsorship confirmation
- Project team introductions
- Scope and objectives overview
- Timeline and major milestones
- Roles and responsibilities clarification

### 2. Strategic Alignment Workshop
- Understand business drivers for modernization
- Define success metrics and KPIs
- Discuss current pain points and challenges
- Identify strategic priorities
- Align on cloud adoption strategies

### 3. Modernization Drivers Documentation
Comprehensive capture of:
- Business drivers (cost, agility, innovation, etc.)
- Technical drivers (scalability, security, reliability)
- Organizational drivers (cultural, skills, process)
- Risk factors and constraints

### 4. Infrastructure Discovery Questionnaire
Baseline assessment of:
- Current infrastructure components
- Data center facilities and capacity
- Networking and security infrastructure
- Compliance and regulatory requirements
- Disaster recovery and business continuity setup

- Strategic business plans (3-5 year outlook)
- Current infrastructure documentation

- Modernization Drivers Document
- Infrastructure Baseline Assessment (from questionnaire)
- Project Governance Structure
- Communication Plan

- ✅ Executive stakeholder alignment on modernization approach
- ✅ Complete and accurate driver documentation

## Dependencies

## Risk Mitigation
| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Lack of executive engagement | Executive sponsorship confirmed in presales |
| Incomplete infrastructure data | Clear questionnaire, follow-up cycles |
| Scope creep | SOW reference for scope boundaries |
| Scheduling conflicts | Executive sponsor addresses team availability |
