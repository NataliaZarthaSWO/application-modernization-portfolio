Executive Summary
This proposal presents SoftwareOne’s AI-Powered Application Modernization Advisory services, designed to support organizations from discovery, assessment, and strategic alignment through the definition of a clear and prioritized application modernization and migration strategy, enabling informed decision-making, risk reduction, and alignment with business objectives.
The purpose of this engagement is to help clients transform their strategic vision into an actionable modernization strategy, enabling them to identify which applications to modernize, how to modernize them, and when to do so—maximizing business value, optimizing technology investment, and establishing a solid foundation for successful execution in subsequent phases.
The assessed applications span legacy and monolithic environments as well as modern enterprise solutions, analyzed from both technical and business perspectives. Through this approach, dependencies, risks, costs, optimization opportunities, and cloud-native adoption capabilities are identified, enabling the definition of target architectures and modernization scenarios aligned with the organization’s future evolution.
To achieve this, SoftwareOne proposes a structured approach aligned with the AI-Powered Application Modernization Lifecycle, focused on the phases of Onboarding, Discovery & Assessment, Strategic Design, and Planning. Throughout this process, artificial intelligence accelerates discovery and assessment activities by synthesizing technical and functional information, identifying patterns, prioritizing initiatives, and consolidating multiple service lines into an integrated modernization and migration strategy. The outcome is translated into clear roadmaps, target architectures, and recommended execution plans.
This advisory enables the definition and validation of the most appropriate approach for each application using the 7R framework (Rehost, Replatform, Refactor, Relocate, Relicense, Retain, or Retire), ensuring that each decision is aligned with the client’s technical, financial, and strategic context, with a strong focus on measurable value and long-term technological sustainability.
Replatform – Move an application to the cloud with some level of optimization to improve scalability, resiliency, and performance.
Refactor – Re-architect and rewrite the application until it is fully suited for the cloud and modern architectures.
Rehost – Move an on-premises application and its dependencies to the cloud with minimal or no changes.
Relicense – Optimize the licensing approach across on-premises and cloud environments when retaining, replacing, or migrating applications.
Retire – Decommission applications that no longer serve any business purpose or add value.
Retain – Keep workloads in their current on-premises environment when modernization is not yet strategic or cost-effective.
Relocate – Transfer entire servers or workloads from an on-premises platform to the cloud version of that platform, maintaining compatibility.
To further strengthen decision-making and reduce uncertainty, the advisory may be complemented by an optional AI-assisted Proof of Value (PoV), designed to validate key hypotheses, demonstrate measurable technical and business impact, and build the confidence required to scale modernization initiatives in a controlled and effective manner.
In its journey to evolve its application portfolio, <CLIENTE> can rely on SoftwareOne to define a clear, prioritized, and business-aligned modernization strategy, leveraging AI-driven insights and cloud-native best practices as the foundation for efficient, value-driven execution in subsequent delivery phases.
