# 05. AI Strategic Analysis

## Overview
This module consolidates findings from Application, Infrastructure, and Database Discovery into an actionable modernization strategy. It defines the target approach, priorities, and business-aligned recommendations that serve as input for PHASE-2 Strategic Design.

## Module Objectives
- Synthesize all Phase 1 discovery outcomes into a single strategic view.
- Prioritize modernization initiatives by business value, risk, and effort.
- Define modernization paths per workload (retain, rehost, replatform, refactor, retire, replace).
- Align technical recommendations with executive and operational stakeholders.
- Produce decision-ready artifacts for architecture, PoV, and business case design.

## Scope
- Consolidation of current-state assessments.
- Strategic analysis of application portfolio and dependencies.
- Workload classification and modernization wave proposal.
- Executive-level recommendation package.

## Key Inputs
- Outputs from `02-AI-Application-Discovery`.
- Outputs from `03-AI-Infraestructure-Discovery`.
- Outputs from `04-AI-DB-Discovery`.
- Stakeholder interviews, constraints, and business priorities.

## Core Activities
1. Review and normalize findings from all discovery tracks.
2. Identify common gaps, blockers, and modernization enablers.
3. Define strategic options and evaluate trade-offs.
4. Build prioritized modernization roadmap (short, mid, long term).
5. Validate recommendations in alignment sessions.
6. Prepare executive narrative and presentation materials.

## Deliverables
- Application Modernization Approach document.
- Strategic approach presentation for business and technology stakeholders.
- Prioritization rationale and sequencing proposal.
- Recommendations to transition into PHASE-2 design modules.

## Templates Available
- `AIPAM-AD-ApplicationModernizationApproach.Template.docx`
	- Main document for strategy definition, options analysis, and recommendations.
- `AIPAM-AD-AppModApproachPresentation.pptx`
	- Executive and steering-committee presentation for strategic alignment.

## Typical Participants
- Engagement Lead / Program Manager
- Enterprise/Cloud Architect
- Application Modernization Consultant
- Database and Infrastructure Specialists
- Customer IT Leadership and Business Sponsors

## Estimated Duration
- 1 to 2 weeks (depends on complexity, data quality, and stakeholder availability).

## Exit Criteria
- Clear modernization approach agreed with stakeholders.
- Prioritized initiative backlog and sequencing ready.
- Strategic decisions documented and approved for PHASE-2.

## Handoff to Next Phase
Outputs from this module become direct inputs for:
- `PHASE-2/06-Cloud-Architecture-Definition`
- `PHASE-2/07-AI-Modernization-PoV`
- `PHASE-2/08-Business-Case-TCO-Modeling`
